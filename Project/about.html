About


Background

It is observed that there is no platform for secondary 6 students in Hong Kong to look for consolidated Universities information in a glance. Every year, there are information days organize by different universities in Hong Kong. Students and parents are busy travelling and going around to look for programmes' information.


Objective

To facilities students and parents when they are looking for programmes' information,by offering centralized, consolidated and simple analysis on university and programme information of universities in Hong Kong, including both UGC-funded and non-UGC-funded universities.
